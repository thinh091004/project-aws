# "AWS Event" Summary Report

### Event Purpose

- Share effective learning methods and practical AI applications, focusing on Prompt Engineering techniques to boost work efficiency.
- Discuss essential skills for freshers in the AI era and introduce the BMAD working framework.
- Gain hands-on experience through prompt optimization demos and direct Q&A with speakers and participants.

### Speaker List

- **Long** - How to get addicted to learning like social media
- **Thịnh** - Automated Prompt Engineering
- **Khang** - "Why haven't you started working?" & "AI-Ready Fresher"
- **Thư** - Introduction to "BMAD" (Benefits & Application)

### Key Highlights

#### Secrets to making learning as "addictive" as social media

- Examine the psychological reasons why the brain prefers social media over acquiring knowledge, based on the contrast between instant gratification and long-term goals.

- Highlight psychological tips to stimulate the brain through variable rewards, combined with task chunking to reduce initial friction and build positive momentum.

- Guide how to leverage social media mechanics to restructure daily habits, transforming learning from passive consumption into an active, engaging process.

#### The art of precise Prompting and cost optimization

- Avoid overly vague prompts while expecting highly detailed and accurate results.

- Optimize prompts to manage token consumption, thereby saving AI operational costs.

- Provide concise, focused context instead of dumping raw data for the AI to filter through.

#### Skill guidance and career Q&A in the AI era

- Discuss and equip freshers with core skill sets to confidently transition into the professional working environment.

- Address direct career development inquiries from students in the auditorium.

- Share practical perspectives on concerns regarding Artificial Intelligence (AI) replacing human workforce.

#### BMAD Framework: Concept, Benefits, and Practical Implementation

- Overview & Benefits: Introduce the BMAD methodology along with its practical value in optimizing productivity, enhancing discipline, and improving work quality.

- Practical Application: Provide step-by-step guidance on implementing BMAD to integrate directly into daily workflows and operations.

### Key Takeaways

- Gain insights into building learning habits using reward mechanisms by breaking down tasks, taking immediate action, and maintaining a consistent pace.

- Learn techniques to craft concise, goal-oriented AI prompts that yield accurate results while minimizing costs.

- Understand core skill requirements for freshers in the AI era, along with working models designed to maximize performance.

### Event Experience

The event delivered highly practical and accessible content. The most impressive parts were the tips for sustaining long-term learning motivation and the cost-conscious prompt engineering techniques. The live demos clearly illustrated the difference in output quality when prompts are properly optimized.
