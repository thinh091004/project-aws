### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The work environment at FCAJ is highly professional, friendly, and open. Throughout my internship, I received enthusiastic support from my mentor and team members whenever I faced challenges. The workspace offers a comfortable atmosphere, making it easy for me to focus and complete my tasks. Furthermore, everyone is always willing to share their experience and create opportunities for interns to learn and grow.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor always provides dedicated guidance, clearly explaining concepts I do not yet understand and encouraging me to take the initiative to research before proposing solutions. This has helped me improve my critical thinking, research, and problem-solving skills. Additionally, the admin team offers prompt support regarding procedures, documentation, and information related to the internship program, allowing me to focus with peace of mind on my learning and the completion of my tasks.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The internship program content is closely aligned with my Information Technology major. It has given me the opportunity to apply the knowledge I have acquired to real-world scenarios while also gaining exposure to new technologies—such as AWS Cloud, Git, and Hugo—and project development workflows. This experience has helped me better understand the professional work environment and enhance my technical expertise.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
The internship program offered numerous opportunities to develop both professional knowledge and soft skills. I honed my abilities in teamwork, time management, corporate communication, and self-directed learning. Through workshops, assignments, and guidance from my mentor, I gained valuable practical experience and a clearer sense of direction for my future career.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The work culture at FCAJ is highly positive and professional. Everyone is respectful and supportive, readily sharing experiences to foster mutual growth. Even as an intern, I felt my opinions were valued; I was entrusted with appropriate tasks and given the opportunity to contribute to the team's activities. This boosted my confidence and motivated me to keep learning.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The internship program offers a flexible schedule that accommodates students' academic commitments, making it easier to balance studies and work. Additionally, participating in training sessions and workshops, along with receiving direct guidance from a mentor, provides practical value by helping me enhance my professional knowledge and skills throughout the internship.


### Additional Questions

- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
Being directly involved in building real-world AWS architecture for a large-scale project (Flash Sale system) rather than just learning theory. The enthusiasm and support from the admin team is something I deeply appreciate.

- **What do you think should be improved for future interns?**  
It would be helpful to have a consolidated reference document with detailed guidelines on system architecture standards. This would help future interns quickly get oriented and standardize their technical diagrams from the very first weeks.

- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why?**  
Absolutely. For me, this is not just a great internship environment — it is a precious opportunity to learn, get familiar with real-world practice, and gain the motivation to keep growing and developing on my career path.
